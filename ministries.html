  • Senior's Ministry

    This group of senior members of Skyway Church meet on the second Tuesday of each month to enjoy each other's company. Always an interesting and informative time with guest speakers and words of encouragement. It's open to all seniors and you are welcome to bring a friend.

  • Children's Ministry

    Trained educators share the Word of God in entertaining and fun ways. Current age groups are from 3 - 10 years old. Starts each Sunday at 10:30 For more information contact Darlene Forsythe

  • Bible Study Ministry

    Faye and Gerard - Wednesday evening Giselle - Friday afternoon

  • Prayer Warrior's Ministry

    The group meets on Wednesday from 2 - 3:30 and prayer is for everyone - contact Muriel or Karla with prayer requests or attend in person.

  • Jeremy Mission Ministry

    Contact Claudine for information regarding this ministry - the focus is on helping those who are homeless by providing warm breakfasts each day of the week.
